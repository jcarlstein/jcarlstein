About

I am currently a fifth year PhD student in the Operations, Information, and Decisions (OID) department at the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School. I am advised by Professor Hamsa Bastani. My research is centered around studying adaptive learning algorithms for healthcare operations. My work thus far has focused on applications to the design of clinical trials and disease pre-screening algorithms, though it is more widely applicable to other areas as well.
