About

I am currently in my fifth year of the Ph.D. program at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. I am advised by Professor Gad Allon. My research has centered on online platforms, specifically focusing on user behavior and how to leverage this behavior to optimize the design of such platforms. This research is tied closely to existing literature on recommender systems and online marketplaces. I am on the 2022-23 academic job market.
