About

I am currently in my fifth year of the Ph.D. program at The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, where I am advised by Professor Gad Allon. My research centers on online platforms, specifically focusing on leveraging user behavior to optimize the design of such platforms, which is tied closely to existing literature on recommender systems and online marketplaces.
